School History

Inventure Education Academy, located in Birtamode-3, Jhapa, Nepal, is a progressive educational institution committed to delivering quality education and holistic development. The school was formed through the merger of two institutionsâ€”Nawodaya Shikshya Sadan and Inventure Kinder House. Prior to this, it was acquired from Nawodaya Funmin School, and initially, the schools operated independently. However, in a major step forward, they were unified under the name Inventure Education Academy to provide a cohesive and modern learning environment. The academy currently serves students from Nursery to Class 10. With a strong emphasis on English-medium instruction and modern technology integration, it stands out as a forward-thinking school in the region. Under the leadership of Founder Principal Mr. Amrit Basnet, the school continues to grow and innovate, aiming to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for success in the 21st century.
